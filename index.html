A Day in the Life

Six Choreographed Acts

The rhythm that turns leadership intent into compounding outcomes. Here is what your day-to-day rituals will look like.

9:30 AM · Act 1

Executive Calendar Planning

Open the leader's day. Review agenda priorities, resolve conflicts, and lock deep-work focus blocks before the office sync begins.

11:00 AM · Act 2

Leadership Meeting Coordination

Align the leadership team, distribute pre-reads, set up presentation decks, and manage timekeeping to ensure every minute is purposeful.

1:00 PM · Act 3

Stakeholder Communication

Triage sensitive communications, respond on behalf of the leader's office, and manage critical updates with investors and board members.

2:30 PM · Act 4

Travel & Logistics Management

Map out domestic and international travel itineraries, coordinate ground transport, bookings, and set up backup plans for travel contingencies.

4:00 PM · Act 5

Priority Alignment Sync

Sit with the CEO/Founders to review active deliverables, unblock outstanding decisions, and escalate urgent challenges requiring prompt action.

6:00 PM · Act 6

Executive Productivity Support

Lock the minutes of meetings, verify action items, compile progress dashboards, and prepare tomorrow's context brief before logging off.

FAQs

Your questions, answered

Where is the work location? +

Finkeda HQ — 11th Floor, M3M Cosmopolitan, Golf Course Ext Rd, Badshahpur, Sector 66, Gurugram, Haryana 122101. This is an on-site role given the close collaboration and proximity required to leadership.

What are typical working hours? +

Standard hours are 9:30 AM – 6:30 PM, Monday to Saturday, with the 2nd and 4th Saturday off. Flexibility is expected for travel windows and time-sensitive leadership commitments.

Notice period considerations? +

We prefer candidates with a 30–60 day notice period. We are open to buyout in exceptional cases for the right fit.

What are the growth opportunities? +

EAs at Finkeda often grow into Chief of Staff, Operations Lead, or Strategy roles. Internal mobility, mentorship and structured career conversations are part of the package.

Will I directly interact with the CEO/Founder? +

Yes. This role reports directly into the office of leadership and you will work 1:1 with senior leadership every single day.
