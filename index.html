COMMON QUESTIONS

The things you're actually wondering.

Do I need AI industry experience to apply? +

Not at all. We care far more about your design thinking and ability to operate with ambiguity than your industry background. Curiosity about AI matters more than a résumé full of AI companies.

Is this role truly remote, or will I be expected in an office? +

Fully remote. We have people in 12 countries. We come together twice a year for team retreats (usually somewhere interesting), but your day-to-day is yours to structure.

What does the design challenge involve? +

It's a real, scoped problem from our product roadmap. We give you 3 days, a brief, and access to any context you need. It's paid — $300 — and if you join, there's a good chance we actually build it.

Is there room to grow into a design leadership role? +

Absolutely. We're building a design team from 1 to 4 in the next 18 months. This hire will likely become our Head of Design once we scale. We're honest about this from day one.

How quickly does the process move? +

We aim for 3 weeks start to finish. We know your time is valuable and we've streamlined everything to avoid the 8-round interview cycles you've probably endured elsewhere.

What if I'm not actively looking but I'm curious? +

Reach out to our Head of Product, Maya, on LinkedIn. No pressure, no aggressive recruiting. If the timing's not right today, we'd love to stay in touch.
